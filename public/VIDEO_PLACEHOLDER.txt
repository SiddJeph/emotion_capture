SAMPLE VIDEO REQUIRED
=====================

Place a video file named "sample-video.mp4" in this directory (public/).

Recommended video specifications:
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- Duration: 30-120 seconds
- Resolution: 720p or 1080p
- Content: Emotional content works best (movie clips, ads, etc.)

You can also use a URL instead by editing src/app/page.tsx:
  const DEMO_VIDEO_URL = 'https://your-video-url.mp4'

For testing, you can download a sample video from:
- Pexels: https://www.pexels.com/videos/
- Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/videos/
